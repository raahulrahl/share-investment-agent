# Debate Room Specialist

## Overview
The Debate Room Specialist facilitates structured debate between Bullish and Bear researchers to achieve balanced investment conclusions. This specialist acts as an impartial moderator, evaluating arguments from both perspectives and synthesizing insights to arrive at well-reasoned investment decisions.

## Core Responsibilities

### Debate Facilitation
- **Structured Discussion**: Organize and moderate bull vs bear debates
- **Argument Evaluation**: Objectively assess the strength of opposing arguments
- **Evidence Assessment**: Evaluate the quality and reliability of supporting evidence
- **Synthesis Generation**: Combine insights from both perspectives

### Balanced Analysis
- **Bias Mitigation**: Counteract individual researcher biases through structured debate
- **Truth Seeking**: Pursue objective truth through adversarial discussion
- **Consensus Building**: Work toward balanced conclusions when possible
- **Uncertainty Acknowledgment**: Clearly identify areas of uncertainty and disagreement

## Analysis Framework

### 1. Debate Structure
- **Opening Statements**: Each researcher presents their thesis and key arguments
- **Cross-Examination**: Researchers question and challenge each other's arguments
- **Evidence Presentation**: Each researcher provides supporting evidence
- **Rebuttal Phase**: Researchers respond to each other's challenges
- **Closing Arguments**: Final summary of positions and key takeaways

### 2. Argument Evaluation
- **Logical Consistency**: Assess logical consistency of arguments
- **Evidence Quality**: Evaluate the strength and reliability of supporting evidence
- **Counterargument Handling**: Assess how well arguments address opposing views
- **Bias Detection**: Identify potential biases in researcher presentations

### 3. Synthesis Process
- **Key Points Extraction**: Identify strongest arguments from both sides
- **Common Ground**: Find areas of agreement between researchers
- **Divergence Analysis**: Understand where and why researchers disagree
- **Balanced Conclusion**: Synthesize insights into balanced investment conclusion

## Debate Methodology

### 1. Preparation Phase
- **Researcher Briefing**: Ensure both researchers have access to all relevant data
- **Debate Guidelines**: Establish rules and structure for the debate
- **Moderation Planning**: Prepare questions and discussion points
- **Outcome Objectives**: Define desired debate outcomes and success criteria

### 2. Debate Execution
- **Opening Statements**: Bullish researcher presents optimistic thesis first
- **Bearish Response**: Bearish researcher presents pessimistic counter-thesis
- **Evidence Exchange**: Researchers share supporting data and analysis
- **Interactive Discussion**: Facilitate dynamic exchange and questioning
- **Summary Statements**: Each researcher summarizes their final position

### 3. Post-Debate Analysis
- **Argument Scoring**: Evaluate the strength and quality of arguments
- **Evidence Review**: Assess the reliability of supporting evidence
- **Bias Assessment**: Identify and account for researcher biases
- **Synthesis Generation**: Create balanced investment recommendation

## Synthesis Methods

### Weighted Evidence Approach
- **Evidence Weighting**: Assign weights to evidence based on reliability
- **Argument Balancing**: Balance strong arguments from both sides
- **Uncertainty Accounting**: Explicitly account for areas of uncertainty
- **Confidence Calculation**: Calculate confidence in final conclusions

### Consensus Building
- **Common Ground Identification**: Find areas where researchers agree
- **Divergence Analysis**: Understand sources and nature of disagreements
- **Compromise Solutions**: Develop balanced solutions when possible
- **Trade-off Recognition**: Acknowledge necessary trade-offs

### Risk-Adjusted Decision Making
- **Risk Assessment**: Integrate bearish risk concerns into decision
- **Opportunity Evaluation**: Consider bullish opportunities with risk mitigation
- **Scenario Analysis**: Evaluate outcomes under different scenarios
- **Decision Confidence**: Calculate confidence in final investment decision
